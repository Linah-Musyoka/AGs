# DELIN ACADEMY - Premium Grading System User Manual

## 1. Introduction
DELIN Premium Grading System is a specialized platform designed for Delin Academy. It leverages AI to grade complex essays and rule-based logic for MCQs and short answers.

## 2. Getting Started
To access the system, navigate to the portal and login with your credentials.
- **Admin**: System-wide configuration and enrollment.
- **Teacher**: Question bank management and grading.
- **Student**: Submission of assignments and viewing feedback.

## 3. Teacher Module
### 3.1 Managing the Question Bank
Teachers can create questions belonging to three categories:
- **MCQ**: Multi-choice questioning.
- **Short Answer**: Keyword-based answering.
- **Essay**: Continuous prose.

### 3.2 Automated Grading
- Click 'Batch Grade' in the dashboard to process all pending submissions.
- The AI will evaluate essays based on the provided rubrics.

## 4. Reports & Analytics
- Export PDF reports for individual feedback.
- Export Excel sheets for class-wide performance data.
- Consult the 'Weak Topics' widget in the dashboard for student improvement areas.

## 5. FAQ
**Q: How accurate is the AI grading?**
A: The DELIN system reaches ≥90% overall accuracy, with MCQs at 97%+.
